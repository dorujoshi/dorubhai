About Arya College

Arya College of Engineering & I.T. was founded in the year 2000, under the patronage of the All India Arya Samaj Society of Higher and Technology Education, with the late Er. T.K. Agarwal Ji as its esteemed Founder and Chairman. Over the years, Arya College has established itself as a prominent private engineering college in Rajasthan, known for its commitment to innovation, excellence, and academic prowess.

Spanning a vast campus, Arya College boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, modern amenities, and a dedication to maintaining the highest educational standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

ACEIT boasts excellent infrastructure and an impressive track record of placements, with over 75% of graduates securing positions in top-tier companies. Additionally, this is the best Engineering College that stands out for its commitment to providing high-quality learning experiences, offering comprehensive and insightful course materials delivered by a team of highly qualified faculty members. The institution also offers a robust support system to ensure students receive the guidance and assistance they need to excel academically and professionally.

Arya College prides itself on delivering outstanding placement opportunities, offering students the chance to secure lucrative positions in top-tier companies. This is the Best B.Tech college in Rajasthan and their placements typically range from an impressive 4 LPA to a staggering 25 LPA, reflecting the institution's commitment to providing students with dream packages. Arya 1st Old Campus welcomed over 150 prestigious companies for recruitment drives, underlining the institution's reputation as a magnet for top-notch employers.

Arya College of Engineering & IT follows the fee structure prescribed by the government of Rajasthan. For B.Tech programs, the fees are set at Rs. 96,104, while M.Tech programs also have a fee of Rs. 96,104. If you're interested in pursuing an MBA, then this is the best MBA College in Rajasthan and the fees for this program are Rs. 75,592. These fee structures ensure accessibility and affordability for aspiring students seeking quality education at Arya College.

ACEIT is proud to house an MSME-approved Arya Incubation Centre dedicated to fostering start-ups. This state-of-the-art facility provides a wealth of resources and support to budding entrepreneurs, including cutting-edge technological infrastructure, initial seed funding, extensive networking opportunities, collaborative co-working spaces, well-equipped laboratories, expert mentoring, and invaluable advisory assistance, among many other offerings. The incubation centre at ACEIT is designed to empower and nurture the entrepreneurial spirit, making it an ideal destination for aspiring start-up founders.

The institute offers a range of career-oriented courses in various disciplines. These programs include:  MBA (Master of Business Administration) | M.Tech (Master of Technology) | B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology). These diverse streams cater to the educational and career needs of students, providing them with opportunities for specialization and skill development in their chosen fields.
